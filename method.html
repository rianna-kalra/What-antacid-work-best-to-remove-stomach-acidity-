Method

1. Add ten drops of grape juice to each beaker.
2. Check the ph level with the ph meter and record.
3. Rinse the eyedropper thoroughly.
4. Add ten drops of lemon juice (an acid) to each beaker that contains the grape juice.
5. Check the ph level with the ph meter and record.
6. Label each beaker with the name of one of the liquid antacids you are testing.
7. Add ten drops of the first antacid you are testing, to the first beaker.
8. Check the ph level with the ph meter and record.
9. Repeat steps 7 and 8 with the remaining types of antacids, adding 10 drops of each antacid to each of the remaining test tubes.
10. Record your data.
